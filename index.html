Welcome to the Darkness

Deep within the misty woods lies an ancient manor, its weathered stone walls bearing witness to centuries of whispered secrets and unspeakable horrors. The air hangs heavy with the scent of decay and forgotten memories, while shadows dance across the cracked windows like specters from another realm.

Legend tells of the manor's original inhabitants, the Blackwood family, who vanished without a trace one fateful autumn night in 1873. Some say they never truly left, their tormented souls bound to the very foundation of this cursed estate. Visitors speak of cold spots that seem to follow you through the halls, and the distant sound of children's laughter echoing from empty rooms.

The manor's architecture itself seems to defy the laws of nature, with corridors that appear to stretch impossibly long and doors that lead to places that shouldn't exist. The wallpaper, once elegant and ornate, now peels away like decaying flesh, revealing the dark history written in the very walls themselves.

Those who have dared to spend a night within these walls speak of vivid nightmares that feel more real than waking life, of figures that move just beyond the edge of vision, and of a pervasive sense of being watched by something ancient and malevolent.
